Data Collection

To ensure the safety of our distribution points and volunteers, FoodRescue collects specific information during account creation.

  • check_circle Verification Documents: We require government ID or organization registration for donors and partners to prevent fraud.
  • check_circle Location Data: Real-time GPS is used during active rescue missions to coordinate pickups and optimize delivery routes.
  • check_circle Communication Logs: Chat logs between donors and volunteers are stored for 90 days to ensure safety and resolve disputes.

Community Guidelines

Zero Tolerance Policy

We strictly prohibit the following behaviors to maintain a safe and productive environment for all members.

person_off
Fake Accounts

Misrepresenting your identity or organization status.

monetization_on
Fraud & Resale

Selling food items received through donations.

campaign
Harassment

Hostile communication toward volunteers or staff.

report_problem
Misinformation

Providing false food quality or quantity reports.

Enforcement Actions

1. Formal Warning

In-app and email notification regarding the specific violation and steps to rectify behavior.

2. Temporary Suspension

7-30 day removal of access to the platform's core matching and pickup features.

3. Permanent Ban

Irrevocable termination of the account and blacklisting of associated verification documents.
