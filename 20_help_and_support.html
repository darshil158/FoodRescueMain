Frequently Asked Questions

What food items can I donate? expand_more
We accept fresh produce, non-perishable canned goods, and prepared foods from licensed kitchens. Please ensure all items are within their expiration dates and stored at proper temperatures before pickup.
How do I track my impact? expand_more
You can view your real-time impact metrics, including meals provided and waste diverted, in the 'Impact Reports' section of your profile dashboard.
What should I do in case of a late pickup? expand_more
If a volunteer is more than 30 minutes late, please use the 'Alerts' tab to notify our dispatch team. We will immediately reassign the route to ensure food safety.
Is my personal data secure? expand_more
Yes, FoodRescue uses end-to-end encryption for all user communications and adheres strictly to regional data protection regulations to ensure your privacy is maintained.
