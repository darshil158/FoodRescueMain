Common Questions

Getting started is easy! Simply download the Food Rescue Hero app, create an account, and complete the quick orientation module. Once verified, you can claim a 'rescue' directly from the home map.

Once you claim a rescue, navigate to the 'My Rescues' tab. You'll see real-time updates for pickup times, delivery locations, and impact metrics. The app uses GPS to guide you through every step.

If you're having trouble logging in or need to update your contact information, visit the Profile tab and select 'Security'. For persistent issues, use the contact form below or our live chat.

If you arrive and the food donor is closed, use the 'Report Issue' button within the active rescue screen. Take a photo of the closed facility if possible, and our dispatch team will handle the rest.
