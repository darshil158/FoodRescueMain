Please read these terms carefully before using the FoodRescue platform. By accessing or using our services, you agree to be bound by these terms.

FoodRescue is a digital facilitation platform designed to connect food donors (restaurants/caterers) with food recipients (NGOs) via a network of logistics volunteers. Our role is strictly as a technical intermediary to minimize food waste and alleviate hunger.

We do not produce, handle, or transport food directly. Our platform serves to coordinate logistics and provide a transparent record of contributions and distributions.

  • Users must be at least 18 years of age to register as a donor, volunteer, or NGO representative.
  • All users must provide valid, verifiable information including government-issued identification where requested.
  • Organizations (Restaurants/NGOs) must provide valid operational licenses and tax identification numbers.

Donors are solely responsible for the safety and quality of the food provided. This includes:

  • Adherence to FSSAI (or local equivalent) food safety standards.
  • Ensuring all food items are within their safe consumption period (not expired).
  • Accurate labeling of potential allergens and storage requirements.
  • Maintaining proper temperature control until the point of volunteer pickup.

Recipients must ensure ethical and safe distribution of received food items:

  • Immediate inspection of food upon arrival.
  • Maintain detailed records of distribution beneficiaries for impact reporting.
  • Prohibit the resale of donated items; all food must be distributed for free.
  • Notification of any safety concerns within 1 hour of receipt.

Volunteers act as independent community members aiding the cause:

  • Adhere to safe driving and traffic laws during transit.
  • Verify pickup and delivery using the OTP system provided in the app.
  • Notify the platform immediately of any spills, accidents, or delays.
  • Treat all donors and recipients with professional courtesy and respect.

FoodRescue reserves the right to:

  • Suspend or terminate accounts for violation of safety protocols or ethical guidelines.
  • Perform random verification checks on FSSAI licenses and NGO registration documents.
  • Modify these terms at any time; users will be notified of significant changes.

To the maximum extent permitted by law, FoodRescue shall not be liable for any food-borne illnesses, accidents, or injuries resulting from the donation process. The platform is provided "as is" without warranty of any kind. Responsibility for food safety lies solely with the Donor and the Recipient.
